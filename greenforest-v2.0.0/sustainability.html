Sustainability



Our Promise

The fast fashion industry promotes constant avalanches of microtrends that last only a couple of weeks before being trumped by the next trend. But where do all these clothes go when they become soooo last week...? 


3 out of 5 of these garments end up in the landfill within a year of being purchased. Less than 1% are recycled, reused or repurposed. To make matters worse, most of them are made of synthetic fabrics (yes, that means they’re made of fossil fuels) that shed thousands of microplastics down the drain and take hundreds of years to decompose. Did you know the fast fashion industry is expected to take up 35% more land (that's 115 MILLION hectares) by 2030? On a planet where we are running out of space at an alarming rate, this expansion will spell disaster for wildlife, and us. Should the destruction of liveable habitats really come at the expense of trends that so often turn to trash?
