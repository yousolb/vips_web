Join Us!


If you make art and want to be part of a group of lovely people that uses crafting for good, or you have another talent that could help further help further the mission, please reach out!

We are always looking for volunteers and would love to grow our community of artists and conservationists. Email our volunteer coordinator at sasha@vipsfund.org or fill out the form below and join the team!

We welcome volunteers in any capacity, from one-time donor artists to full-time new members of our crochet club at the University of Michigan. We are also hoping to expand to other college campuses, so please reach out if you would be interested in starting a VIP's branch at your institution!
